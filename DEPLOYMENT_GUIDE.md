# Final Indexing & Deployment Guide - Fortune Mutuma Kigunda

To ensure your portfolio appears at the **very top** of search results for your name, follow these final deployment steps. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Search engines like Google take time to "discover" new sites. Following these steps will speed up the process from weeks to hours.

## 1. Google Search Console (Crucial)
This is the most important step for ranking #1.
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/).
2. Add your site URL as a "Property" (e.g., `https://alphadoane.github.io/fortune/`).
3. Verify ownership (GitHub Pages verification is usually automatic or via a small file).
4. Go to **Sitemaps** in the sidebar.
5. Enter `sitemap.xml` and click **Submit**.
6. Google will now actively crawl and index your name.

## 2. Force Indexing (The Shortcut)
If you want to appear in results **today**:
1. In Search Console, use the **URL Inspection** tool at the top.
2. Paste your homepage URL.
3. Click **Request Indexing**. This tells Google to prioritize your site immediately.

## 3. Social Media "Backlinks"
Google ranks sites higher when they are linked from "High Authority" domains.
1. **LinkedIn**: Add your portfolio link to your "Contact Info" and "Featured" section.
2. **GitHub**: Add the link to your GitHub profile website field.
3. **Twitter/X**: Add it to your bio.

## 4. HTTPS (Security)
Google penalizes "Insecure" sites.
- If using GitHub Pages, ensure **"Enforce HTTPS"** is checked in the repository settings under **Pages**.

## 5. Image Search Ranking
Your profile image is already optimized with appropriate ALT tags. Once Google indexes the site, searching your name in **Google Images** should also feature your professional portrait at the top.
